## What is OMT?
OMT is a machine-interpretable semantic framework engineered for measurement science. Built upon international standards such as VIM (International Vocabulary of Metrology) and GUM (Guide to the Expression of Uncertainty in Measurement), OMT is designed to bridge the semantic gap in the data exchange layer of Digital Calibration Certificates (DCC).
## Why OMT？
- Eliminating Ambiguity: Utilizing a Controlled Vocabulary (CV) mechanism, OMT assigns unique, globally recognized metrological semantics to every XML node within a DCC.
- Semantic Anchor: OMT transforms flat calibration data into deeply interconnected Knowledge Graphs, enabling machines to perceive the logical relationships between measurement concepts.
